The Story Behind
Match & Snap

Where memory meets creativity, and every moment becomes a keepsake.

Our Beginning

Match & Snap was born from a simple observation — the most joyful digital experiences combine play with creativity. What started as a university project evolved into a passion project dedicated to bringing smiles through pixel-perfect memories.

Every feature, from the satisfying card flips to the delicate photo filters, is crafted with intention. We believe that technology should feel warm, not cold — and that creative tools should be accessible to everyone.

🌸
✨

Our Mission

To create a digital space where playfulness and aesthetics coexist — a place where you can challenge your memory, express your creativity, and collect moments that make you smile.

👩‍🎨

Meet the Creator

I'm a designer and developer who believes that the web should be beautiful, intuitive, and delightful. Match & Snap is my love letter to thoughtful design — a project where every animation, every color, and every interaction has been carefully considered.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me sketching, collecting vintage postcards, or exploring quiet cafes. I hope this little corner of the internet brings you as much joy as it brought me to create it.

— with warmth, from Amsterdam
